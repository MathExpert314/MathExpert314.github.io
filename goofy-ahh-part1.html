The Nether Showdown: Mario vs. Doof vs. Shrek (featuring confusion)

Universe 1: The Mushroom Kingdom

Mario was enjoying his daily practice of punching bricks with his forehead and yelling "WAHOO!" when the sky opened like a ravioli. A swirling vortex sucked him in mid-jump, right as he yelled,

"Let’s-a gaaaaaaaaAAAAAAH—"

Universe 2: Tri-State Area

Dr. Doofenshmirtz had just finished building his latest “-inator”:

“The Spontaneous Random Combatant Summoner-inator™”

He turned to Dug, who was wearing a lab coat for no reason, and said,

“With this, I can summon someone to fight Perry the Platypus FOR ME!”

The machine exploded into a portal, sucking them both in.

Dug: “I was promised walkies...”

Universe 3: Far Far Away

Shrek and Donkey were arguing about onion rings when a glowing red Nether portal erupted from their outhouse and sucked them in mid-banter.

Shrek: “SOMEBODY ONCE TOLD MEEEE—!”

The Nether - The Lava-Filled Cage Match Arena

Mario ground-pounded a Magma Cube. Doof and Dug landed on a floating obsidian rock. Shrek and Donkey rode in on a flaming Ghast.

Shrek: “Why are we in Minecraft Hell?”

Donkey: “I don’t know, but I’m about to clap some plumber cheeks.”

Round One: Total Chaos

Mario blasted fireballs. Shrek ate one and burped lava. Dug entered HyperFetch Mode. Donkey threw a Nether wart that exploded into sound effects. It was insane.

Doof: “MY SCIENTIFICALLY AVERAGE KNEES!”

Enter: Kris and Susie (Deltarune)

Kris and Susie walk through a calm portal. Susie stares at the madness.

“What the hell is this lava Thunderdome?”

They see Shrek yeet a Blaze, Mario and Donkey fistfighting on a strider, and Dug glowing like a god-dog.

Susie: “Best. Sidequest. Ever.”

Epilogue: Peanut Butter Surf Session 5000

Donkey accidentally activates Peace Mode. The lava becomes chunky, eldritch peanut butter.

Shrek: “I... don’t know if I’m mad or hungry.”

Mario: “MAMMA MIA!”

Doof: “DUG. We are starting the FIRST EVER PEANUT BUTTER SURFING LEAGUE.”

Dug: “I do not know what surfing is, but I love you.”

They surf the peanut butter sea as flaming banjo skeletons play Free Bird.

Shrek: “...Is it just me or is this kind of beautiful?”

Susie: “I’m not dealing with this crap.”
